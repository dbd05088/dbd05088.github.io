Byeonghwi Kim (김병휘)

I'm a Ph.D. student at Vision and Learning Lab. at Yonsei University advised by Prof. Jonghyun Choi. My research focus is on embodied AI.

During my M.S., I was fortunate to work as a research intern at the PRIOR team in Allen Institute of Artificial Intelligence (AI2) (2021-2022). Currently, I am looking for a research internship position for embodied agent research.

I received a M.S. degree from Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology under the supervision of Prof. Jonghyun Choi and a B.S. degree with first-class honours from Kwangwoon University under the supervision of Prof. Cheolsoo Park.
